# Project Overview: Football Coaching Marketplace Platform

## 1. Product Vision
The platform is a football-only coaching marketplace and player development platform where players and parents discover private football coaches, book and pay for training sessions, and maintain a portable long-term player development history.

It uniquely combines:

*   **Marketplace Discovery:** Finding coaches by location and specialization.
*   **Coach Business Management:** Scheduling, billing, and client management for independent trainers.
*   **Development Exposure Tracking:** Automatically estimating skill-specific training load (SLUs) instead of simple time logging.
*   **Parent Engagement:** Providing visibility into progress and managing underage players safely.
*   **Video-Based Workflows:** Supporting drill demonstrations, homework uploads, and coach feedback.
*   **Subscription Monetization:** Recurring revenue models for both coaches and players.

The platform is designed for **independent football coaches**, moving away from traditional club or academy management structures.

## 2. Product Goals

### Primary Goals
1.  **Marketplace Growth:** Facilitate coach discovery by location and specialization.
2.  **Professional Management:** Empower coaches to run their private training businesses professionally.
3.  **Long-Term Tracking:** Provide a persistent, portable record of player development.
4.  **Revenue Generation:** Establish recurring subscription revenue from coaches and players.
5.  **User Retention:** Build high retention through progress tracking and historical data.
6.  **Global Readiness:** Support multilingual operations, starting with English and German.

### Secondary Goals
1.  **Efficiency:** Reduce administrative overhead for coaches.
2.  **Communication:** Streamline interactions between coaches, players, and parents.
3.  **Trust:** Increase professionalism and trust in the private coaching sector.
4.  **Scalability:** Build a foundation suitable for future native mobile applications.

## 3. Success Metrics (KPIs)
Based on the project goals, success will be measured by:

*   **Active Coaches:** Number of verified independent coaches using the platform.
*   **Booking Volume:** Number of successfully completed and paid training sessions.
*   **Subscription Conversion:** Percentage of users converting from free/basic tiers to Pro/Elite tiers.
*   **Retention Rate:** Duration of active player-coach relationships and history maintenance.
*   **Development Engagement:** Usage frequency of the "Skills Radar," "SLU tracking," and homework features.
*   **Platform Revenue:** Total commission from marketplace transactions and subscription fees.

## 4. Target Users
*   **Coaches:** Independent trainers needing client acquisition and professional tracking tools.
*   **Parents:** Primary payers managing schedules and progress visibility for underage players.
*   **Players:** Athletes receiving coaching, completing drills, and tracking their personal growth.
*   **Administrators:** Internal users managing moderation, disputes, and platform operations.

## 5. Core Values
*   **Safety & Compliance:** High priority on minor protection (GDPR, age-based restrictions).
*   **Data Integrity:** Reliable skill load calculations (SLU) and assessment history.
*   **Technical Excellence:** Performant video handling, accurate timezone management, and secure payment processing.
